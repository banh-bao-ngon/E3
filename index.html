LAB MONITORING

Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (aPTT)

  • Draw STAT aPTT q6hrs after starting drip
  • Round aPTT result to nearest whole number
  • Adjust DOSE based on protocol chart

aPTT Timing

  • Dose change = count 6 hours from time of dose adjustment
  • No change = count 6 hours from aPTT result
  • After 2 consecutive aPTTs at goal range, switch to q24hrs
  • If daily aPTT is out of range, restart aPTT every 6 hours.

Discontinuing aPTTs

  • Discontinue aPTT draws once heparin is stopped.

CBC

  • Draw CBC with platelets the day after heparin starts.
  • Then, every 3 days minimum while on heparin.

If unable to draw labs or the patient refuses, notify the provider immediately.
